Our Story

MeisterCo. was founded in 2010 with a simple yet ambitious vision: to revolutionize the automotive retail experience in Malaysia. What started as a small family-owned dealership has grown into one of the region's most trusted Perodua and Proton dealers.
"We believe that buying a car should be an exciting journey, not a stressful ordeal." - Muhammad Atif, Founder & CEO
From our humble beginnings in Puchong, we've built our reputation on three fundamental principles: transparency, quality, and genuine care for our customers. Every vehicle we sell comes with our personal guarantee of excellence, backed by comprehensive after-sales support that extends far beyond the initial purchase.
